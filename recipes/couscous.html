Vegetable Couscous

A flavorful and healthy Moroccan dish made with couscous and mixed vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup couscous
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 1 zucchini, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup raisins (optional)
  • Chopped cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add onions, carrots, zucchini, and bell pepper. Cook until soft.
  3. Stir in cumin, turmeric, salt, and pepper, and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  4. In a separate bowl, prepare the couscous by adding it to boiling water. Let it steam for about 5 minutes, then fluff with a fork.
  5. Combine the cooked vegetables with couscous and mix well.
  6. Optional: Add raisins for sweetness and garnish with chopped cilantro.
