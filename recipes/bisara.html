Bisara

A comforting Moroccan split pea soup, perfect for cold days. It's simple, healthy, and packed with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried split peas
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (around 4 cups)
  • Optional: chili flakes, extra olive oil for garnish

Steps

  1. Rinse the split peas and place them in a pot with water.
  2. Add garlic, cumin, paprika, and salt.
  3. Cook over medium heat until the peas are soft, stirring occasionally.
  4. Blend the soup with an immersion blender (or mash by hand) until smooth.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle chili flakes if desired.
  6. Serve hot with warm bread or a spoon of harissa on the side.
